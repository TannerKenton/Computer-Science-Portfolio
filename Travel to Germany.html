Travel to...

Germany

Germany is full of culture, food, and other amazing things.

One of Germany's biggest features are the huge amount of castles. One example of these castles is the:

Hohenzollern Castle


Germany is also full of many other amazing things. For example, Germany makes some of the best food in the world.

Some examples of the great food in Germany are:

  1. Bratwurst
  2. Apple Strudel
  3. Black Forest Cherry Cake
  4. Schnitzel
Germany also contains some of the most interesting animals. For instance, one of the endangered animals of Germany is the:

Lynx

The rivers are also filled with salmon and carp that fall prey to the brown bears.

Some other amazing animals that live in Germany are:
  1. Golden Eagle
  2. Gray Wolf
  3. Wild Boars
  4. Elk
  5. Moutain Hare
...and many others.

Germany has many other great things to offer and should be your #1 place to visit. The amazing landscapes, tourist locations, and food are some of the main reasons you should make Germany your next travel location.
